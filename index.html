About TutorDeck

TutorDeck is a soon-to-be nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering high school students to become mentors and tutors for their peers and middle schoolers.

🎯

Our Mission

To create a peer-to-peer learning network that empowers students to help students, fostering academic excellence and leadership skills.

🌟

Our Vision

A world where every student has access to quality peer tutoring and mentorship, breaking down barriers to educational success.

🤝

Our Impact

Building confidence, improving grades, and creating lasting mentorship relationships that benefit both tutors and students.

🌍

Global Reach

Through Schoolhouse.world, we connect students across continents, breaking geographical barriers to education.
